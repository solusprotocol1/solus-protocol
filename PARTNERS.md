## Why Partner with S4 Ledger?

- **Defense Focus:** Purpose-built for defense logistics verification — supply chains, maintenance, technical data, CDRLs
- **Compliance:** NIST 800-171 and CMMC aligned — zero data on-chain
- **Transparency:** All anchors are independently verifiable on the public XRP Ledger
- **Low Cost:** ~$0.001 per anchor, 3-5 second confirmation
- **Open Source:** Full SDK source code available for audit and integration
