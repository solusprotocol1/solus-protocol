About

Built by defense logistics professionals

We see supply chain, maintenance, and data integrity problems firsthand. S4 Ledger exists because we lived the pain.

Our Mission

Replace legacy logistics tools with one auditable platform

S4 Ledger is a full Integrated Logistics Support (ILS) workspace — not just an audit trail. It covers all 12 ILS elements defined in MIL-STD-1388, giving defense teams 20+ tools in a single platform:

• ILS Gap Analysis (MIL-STD-1388-2B)
• DMSMS / Obsolescence Tracker
• Readiness & RAM Calculator
• Compliance Scorecard
• Supply Chain Risk Engine (AI)
• Action Items Manager
• Predictive Maintenance (AI)
• Lifecycle Cost Estimator
• ROI Calculator
• Audit Vault & Digital Thread
• Defense Document Library
• Report Generator
• Submissions & PTD Review
• SBOM Viewer

Every analysis, record, and report produced inside the workspace can be cryptographically hashed and anchored to the XRP Ledger — creating tamper-proof audit trails that anyone can independently verify, without vendor lock-in.
