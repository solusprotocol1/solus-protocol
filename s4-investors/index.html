The investment case for immutable defense logistics verification

The Opportunity

The U.S. Department of Defense spends over $400 billion annually on contracts, logistics, and supply chain management. Counterfeit parts alone cost the DoD an estimated $2-3 billion per year. Failed audits, version disputes, maintenance falsification, and contract deliverable disagreements add billions more in waste, rework, and risk.

There is currently no widely adopted, cost-effective, independently verifiable solution for immutable logistics record verification in defense. S4 Ledger fills that gap.
