How It Works

Four steps to tamper-proof records

Every defense record gets a cryptographic fingerprint anchored to a public, decentralized ledger. No data leaves your system.

01 — Event
Record created
Part received, maintenance completed, CDRL delivered, or any logistics event occurs.
02 — Hash
SHA-256 fingerprint
A one-way cryptographic hash is computed locally. No data content leaves your environment.
03 — Anchor
Stored on XRPL
The hash is written immutably to the XRP Ledger—confirmed in 3–5 seconds for $0.01.
04 — Verify
Independent proof
Anyone can re-hash the original and compare against the public ledger. No vendor required.
