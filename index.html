Why the XRP Ledger?

We evaluated every major blockchain — XRPL is the only one that meets defense requirements

3-5 Second Finality
Ethereum takes 12+ minutes for finality. Solana has suffered multiple outages. XRPL has settled in 3-5 seconds with 99.99% uptime since 2012 — over 13 years of uninterrupted operation.
$0.01 Per Anchor
Ethereum gas fees range $5–$50+ per transaction. Private blockchains cost $500K+ in infrastructure. XRPL base fees are a fraction of a cent — S4 Ledger anchors cost just $0.01 SLS, making high-volume defense anchoring economically viable.
Public & Neutral
Private blockchains (Hyperledger, Guardtime KSI) are controlled by a single vendor — defeating the purpose of independent verification. XRPL is public and decentralized with 150+ independent validators. No vendor lock-in. Ever.
No Mining, No Waste
XRPL uses a federated consensus protocol — not proof-of-work (Bitcoin) or proof-of-stake (Ethereum). No energy-intensive mining. Each transaction uses negligible power, aligning with federal sustainability mandates.
Quantum-Resistant Architecture
SHA-256 hashes stored on XRPL are one-way fingerprints — even Grover's algorithm only reduces security from 2^256 to 2^128, still computationally infeasible. S4 Ledger monitors NIST post-quantum standards.
Independently Verifiable
Anyone can verify an S4 Ledger anchor using the public XRPL explorer — no proprietary software, no vendor account, no subscription required. Re-hash the original data and compare. That's it.
