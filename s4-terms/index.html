2. Description of Service

S4 Ledger provides an immutable logistics verification platform for the defense industry. The Service computes SHA-256 hashes of defense logistics records and anchors those hashes to the XRP Ledger (XRPL), creating tamper-proof audit trails.

Key Technical Facts

  • No sensitive data on-chain: Only 64-character SHA-256 hashes are stored on the public ledger. No Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI), Personally Identifiable Information (PII), classified data, or ITAR-controlled content is written to the blockchain.
  • Hash anchoring: Records are hashed client-side or via our API. The hash is anchored to an XRPL transaction memo field.
  • $SLS Token: A utility token issued on the XRP Ledger used to pay per-anchor micro-fees. $SLS is not equity, a security, or an investment contract.
