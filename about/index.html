Who We Are

SLS Protocol is an early-stage blockchain startup headquartered in Mount Pleasant, South Carolina. Founded by a solo developer with deep expertise in blockchain technology and healthcare data challenges, we are creating a decentralized protocol on the XRP Ledger to set a new standard for secure, patient-controlled medical data integrity.

Our focus is real-world utility: immutable hash anchoring for medical records, zero-knowledge privacy (PHI never touches the chain), and seamless integration with existing EHR systems ‚Äî all built with transparency, compliance, and long-term scalability in mind.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower patients with true ownership and control over their medical data while enabling healthcare providers to access accurate, verifiable records in a compliant, efficient, and tamper-proof manner ‚Äî beginning with a focused rollout in South Carolina to create meaningful local economic and health impact before expanding nationally.

We believe the future of healthcare depends on trust in data. SLS Protocol exists to make that trust immutable, decentralized, and accessible to everyone.

Why "Solus"?

We chose 'Solus' from the Latin word meaning 'alone' or 'only' because our protocol empowers patients to have sole control over the integrity of their medical data. In a fragmented healthcare system full of intermediaries, Solus creates a single, unbreakable source of truth on the XRPL ‚Äî unique, independent, and patient-centered. It's not about storing data; it's about making each record's authenticity stand alone, verifiable by anyone, forever.

Our Impact

By solving the problems of fragmented and insecure healthcare data, SLS Protocol aims to significantly reduce the estimated $300 billion annual cost of data mismanagement in the US healthcare system. In South Carolina, we are prioritizing partnerships and pilots with institutions like the Medical University of South Carolina (MUSC), which serves over 2.4 million patients statewide, to drive innovation, improve care coordination, and create lasting economic value in the region.

Long-term, our goal is to become the global standard for decentralized medical data integrity, enabling safer, faster, and more equitable healthcare worldwide.
