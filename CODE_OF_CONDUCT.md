# S4 Ledger — Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the S4 Ledger community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Defense Information Handling

S4 Ledger operates in the defense logistics space. Contributors must:

- **Never** include classified, CUI, ITAR-controlled, or export-controlled information in any contribution, issue, or discussion
- **Never** include real contract numbers, NSNs tied to sensitive programs, or personnel information
- Use only fictional or publicly available example data in documentation and test cases
- Report any accidental disclosure immediately to security@s4ledger.com

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to info@s4ledger.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
