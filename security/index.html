Security Philosophy

Solus Protocol is built with security as the foundational principle. We employ "Privacy-by-Design" architecture, ensuring Protected Health Information (PHI) is never stored or transmitted on-chain. Only cryptographic SHA-256 hashes are anchored to the XRP Ledger, providing immutable proof of integrity without compromising patient privacy.

All components undergo rigorous threat modeling, and we maintain an open responsible disclosure program to collaborate with the security research community.

HIPAA & Regulatory Compliance

Solus Protocol is designed to support HIPAA compliance by keeping all sensitive PHI off-chain in provider-controlled, encrypted storage. Hash anchoring on XRPL provides verifiable audit trails without exposing protected data.

Current steps include:

  • Off-chain encryption (AES-256) and local storage for PHI
  • Hash-only on-chain anchoring for integrity proof
  • Planned third-party HIPAA audits and certification
  • Business Associate Agreement (BAA) readiness for pilot partners

We are actively preparing for full regulatory alignment, including GDPR considerations for future international expansion.

Legal & Token Framework

$SLS is a pure utility token designed for protocol operations (data anchoring fees, incentives, governance). It confers no equity, dividends, or profit-sharing rights and is not intended as an investment contract.

We are obtaining formal legal opinions to confirm $SLS's utility classification under applicable securities laws. All marketing and usage emphasize functional access and network participation, not speculative investment.
