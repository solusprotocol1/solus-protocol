How S4 Ledger Works

SHA-256 Hashing

Every record is hashed client-side using the SHA-256 algorithm. The hash is a unique 64-character fingerprint тАФ change one bit of the original, and the hash changes completely.

XRPL Anchoring

The hash is embedded in an XRPL Memo field on a real blockchain transaction. Once confirmed by validators, the record is permanent and tamper-proof тАФ nobody can alter it.

SLS Token Economy

Each anchor costs 0.01 SLS (~$0.0001). SLS is the utility token that powers every action. Subscriptions include SLS allocations based on your tier.

Defense-Grade Security

Compliant with NIST 800-171, DFARS, ITAR, and CMMC Level 3. Built specifically for defense logistics integrity and chain of custody.
